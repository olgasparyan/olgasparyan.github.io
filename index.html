I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Political Science at Florida State University. I received my PhD in Political Science from the University of Rochester and held a Postdoctoral Fellow position at the Data Science Lab at Hertie School, working with the SCRIPTS Cluster of Excellence and CATALYSE EU Horizon Project.

My research lies in the fields of comparative political economy, public policy, and visual studies, and is conducted with an application of causal inference and machine learning and deep learning models.

I am a member of the Lancet Countdown team, where I contribute by computationally analyzing climate change rhetoric of the public, media, and international organizations. My work has appeared in Political Analysis, Political Science Research and Methods, The Lancet, and Lancet Public Health.
